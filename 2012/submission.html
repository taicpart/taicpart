PAPER SUBMISSION

You submit your paper by uploading a PDF to EasyChair: https://www.easychair.org/conferences/?conf=taicpart2012. You need to create an account if you don't already have one.

Logging in as an author, you will be asked to provide:

  1. The list of authors with their personal data
  2. The title of the paper
  3. The abstract of the paper (plain text)
  4. A list of up to 5 keywords
  5. The paper itself in pdf format

Please provide a meaningful abstract and carefully choose keywords since they are important in assigning reviewers to your work.

By submitting a paper to TAIC PART, authors warrant that the work is original and that the paper or a similar contribution is neither published nor considered for publication elsewhere.

FORMATTING GUIDELINES

The initial submission should be done in pdf format. We invite submissions of the following types:

  • Short papers (6 pages): Experience reports, research methods, longer challenge papers
  • Fast Abstracts (2 pages): Work in progress, challenges

Please check the Call for Papers on detailed information on the submission types.

Papers must conform to the two-column IEEE conference publication format (http://www.computer.org/portal/web/cscps/formatting), and must be submitted in pdf format. We recommend that you use this format for preparing your initial submission. The maximum number of pages is based on this format.
